Gives to non-members of a group the possibility of knowing the membership of a group.

The members of the group must have the {@link appia.protocols.remoteView.GossipOutExtLayer} in a channel. Also the {@link appia.protocols.remoteView.RemoteViewLayer} must be in a channel of the application interested in knowing the membership.

In order to take advantage of this functionality a gossip server must be running for the group.

The requests are sent trough a {@link appia.protocols.remoteView.RemoteViewEvent}
